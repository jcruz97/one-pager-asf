We fly where nobody can

We never get down if our help is required, bad weather or long flights can't tame our motivation. Even if it's dangerous, we will head to people that need medical supplies, food or even books.
We also carry members of other associations such as the Red Cross to reach places that can't be reached by classic ways of transportation.
